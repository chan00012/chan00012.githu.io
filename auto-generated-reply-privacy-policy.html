Privacy Policy for auto-generated-reply

Effective Date: July 24, 2025

auto-generated-reply (“the App”) respects your privacy. This privacy policy outlines how we handle information when you use our app, especially in connection with Meta products such as Facebook or Messenger.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or process any personal data from users. The App only connects to Facebook Business Suite’s Messenger API for the purpose of creating automated replies based on keyword triggers.

2. How We Use Data

Since we do not collect or store user data, we do not use any personal information for any purpose.

3. Use of Meta APIs

The App uses Meta’s Messenger API only to:

  • Read messages sent to the Facebook page
  • Send automated keyword-based replies

No message data is stored, logged, or shared by our servers. All processing is handled within the automation logic of the platform (e.g., n8n) and is transient in nature.

4. Data Sharing

We do not share any information with third parties.

5. Data Retention

Since we do not collect or store any data, there is no data retained.

6. User Controls

As no personal data is collected, there are no user accounts or identifiable information to manage or delete.

7. Security

Although we do not store data, we ensure secure communication with Meta’s APIs using encrypted channels (HTTPS).
